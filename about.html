Who We Are

If you take a look into our history as an organisation. You’d find out that we a fierce leader in the active and prolonged fight against cancer. Since 1931, our inception, we've always looked for more innovative ways to combat cancer, saving more and more people’s lives.

Why We're Doing What We're Doing

We aspire, with every fibre in our being, to lead South africa towards a cancer free society.
We believe it is possible through thorough education and thorough research. Making the lives of other people, people that walk among you, all the better.
